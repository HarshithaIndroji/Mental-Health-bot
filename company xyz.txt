Mental health can be affected by a number of factors, including genetics, social factors, and development. Here are some ways to improve mental health: 
Treatment
A mental health professional can create an individualized treatment plan that may include therapy, medication, or other treatments. 
Lifestyle changes
Eating well, exercising, and getting enough sleep can help improve mental health. 
Self-care
Practicing gratitude, setting goals, and taking breaks from negative information can help. 
Connecting with others
Spending time with friends and family, volunteering, and helping others can improve mental health. 
Relaxation
Trying relaxation techniques like meditation, muscle relaxation, or breathing exercises can help. 
Coping skills
Developing coping skills can help stay positive. 
Mental health professionals include psychiatrists, psychologists, licensed professional clinical counselors, social workers, nurse practitioners, and family physicians.
